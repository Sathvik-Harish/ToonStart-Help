Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: My animation isn't playing, or it's playing too fast/slow.

A: Check your FPS (Frames Per Second) slider to adjust the speed. Also, ensure you have multiple frames with distinct content to see movement.

Q: Why can't I see my entire drawing on the canvas?

A: Use the Pan (‚úã) tool to move the canvas view, or try adjusting your browser's zoom level.

Q: Where are my saved projects stored?

A: Your projects are saved locally in your browser's storage. This means they are tied to the specific browser and device you used to save them. Clearing your browser's data (cookies, cache) may delete your saved projects.

Troubleshooting & Common Issues

Lagging Performance:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection (if applicable).
  • Close other demanding browser tabs or applications.
  • For very complex animations, consider breaking them into smaller parts.

Exported GIF is Low Quality/Too Large:

  • The quality and file size of GIFs are highly dependent on the number of frames, colors, and resolution. Try reducing the number of frames or simplifying your artwork.

Palm Rejection isn't working:

  • Make sure the Palm Rejection toggle is switched to On. Also, ensure your stylus is properly connected and recognized by your device.

My previously saved project isn't loading.

  • Ensure you are using the same browser and same device that you used to save the project. If you've cleared your browser's cache or data, local saves may have been removed.

Tips & Tricks

  • Keyboard Shortcuts: While ToonStart is designed for touch and mouse, explore browser-level shortcuts like Ctrl/Cmd + Z for Undo (browser-wide).
  • Layering with Frames: Create depth and movement by subtly changing elements across multiple frames. Use Duplicate to make small adjustments.
  • Experiment with FPS: Play around with the FPS slider to achieve different animation styles, from fast-paced action to slow, deliberate movements.
  • Use Generate Idea often: Don't hesitate to use the Generate Idea feature even if you have a plan; it might give you a fresh perspective!
